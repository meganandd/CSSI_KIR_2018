My Experience

I have always been interested in art since a young age. Needlework, sewing, knitting, embroidery, and crocheting have been a big part of my family for years, and I loved learning these new skills as I got older. I recently learned how to crochet because I wanted to make a lap blanket for my friend before she leaves for college in August. I started simple, and chose a pattern that only uses 'chaining' and single-crochets. It looked complicated at first, but as I soon as I found my rhythm, it became very relaxing and a fun new pasttime. I hope you learn to love it just as much as I do!
